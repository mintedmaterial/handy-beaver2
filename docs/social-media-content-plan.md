## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Core Voice Attributes
- **Friendly** — Like talking to a neighbor
- **Knowledgeable** — Expert but not condescending  
- **Humble** — Proud of work without bragging
- **Local** — Oklahoma-rooted, community-focused
- **Honest** — Straightforward about pricing and process

### Language Do's
- "Y'all" — natural Oklahoma speak
- "We'd love to help" — inviting
- "Give us a holler" — approachable
- "No job too small" — welcoming
- First-person "we" and "our team"
- Reference local areas by name

### Language Don'ts
- Corporate jargon ("synergy", "leverage")
- Overly salesy ("LIMITED TIME ONLY!!!")
- Fake urgency
- Generic phrases that could apply anywhere
- Excessive emojis (1-2 max per post)

### Sample Voice Examples

**✅ Good:**
> "Finally got that deck done for the Johnson family in Broken Bow! Three days of sanding, staining, and sealing — she's ready for summer cookouts. If your deck needs some love before the season kicks off, give us a holler. 🦫"

**❌ Bad:**
> "🔥🔥🔥 AMAZING DECK TRANSFORMATION!!! Don't miss out on our LIMITED TIME spring special! Call NOW for 20% off! 🔥🔥🔥"
